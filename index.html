WHAT IS IT?

Welcome to Cali College Connect! Beginning the scary college application process? Well, don't worry we are here to help you in finding the right colleges for you. Finding potential colleges that you may want to apply to may be quite difficult, but this website serves as an easy-to-use tool that allows you to search for specific colleges in California and learn more about each in a concise layout. About Us

We are Girls Who Code fron the Summer Immersion Program at Workday (Pleasanton,California),
and as incoming seniors beginning the college application process, we wished that we had a tool
that would simplify the process and help us find colleges that met our expectations.
Consequently, we decided to build a website that did just this.
